Simulation Overview

BioBalance: Digestive Control is an interactive educational simulation designed to help students understand the processes of digestion and nutrient absorption in the human body. The game integrates structured learning phases, system-based interactions, and quiz-based reinforcement to promote active learning in biology classrooms.

Navigate through four critical phases: Cephalic activation, Gastric processing, Duodenal homeostasis, and Villi absorption. Each phase features real-time feedback mechanisms, metabolic tracking, and mastery-based progression systems.

About BioBalance

BioBalance: Digestive Control is a free interactive educational simulation created to help high school and college students better understand the human digestive system.

It features four immersive phases (Cephalic, Gastric, Duodenal, and Villi), mastery tracking, and quiz-based reinforcement — designed for classroom use or self-paced learning.

Built as a project with a focus on creating an engaging, and offline-friendly biology education.
